Masculinity:

Masculinity refers to the set of characteristics and behaviors that are traditionally associated with being a man. While the specific traits and behaviors that are considered masculine can vary across cultures and over time, some ubiquitous features of masculinity include strength, competitiveness, assertiveness, and emotional resilience. Historically, masculinity has been closely linked with power and dominance, particularly in patriarchal societies where men have held positions of authority. This has led to the development of a range of cultural norms and expectations surrounding what it means to be a “real man”. Masculinity simply means you, i.e. a true man.

It has been observed that men fall short of being actual men these days. Men these days have lost their identity. They forgot what it meant to be a man, a man means a true man. They lost their power and culture. Traditional men used to face their problems and solve them on their own. Men used to be warriors both physically and mentally. But lots of men of the modern age are too weak to face their problems. Lack of masculinity is the root of suicide. They commit suicide in the name of minor things like heartbreak. There is nothing as people think. We are men, so it is our power to face the problems, not to run away. You are not a true man if you run away from challenges. A few decades back, society was male dominant. Now, in the modern world, a man must be able to respect and accept the opposite gender. Men have lost their confidence. Every man must realize who they are!!

Every man should be powerful as a warrior. We become who we build ourselves to be. A person who clearly understands what masculinity refers to never runs away from facing challenges. Ultimately, involves a commitment to personal growth and self-improvement. Men who strive to be their best selves, both mentally and physically, are better equipped to handle life’s challenges and create a positive impact on the world around them. When men embrace positive masculinity traits, they can contribute greatly to society, build meaningful relationships, and inspire those around them.
